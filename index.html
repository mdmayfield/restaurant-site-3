Located along historic Arcadia Bay Avenue with a beautiful scenic view of the Bay itself, the Two Whales Diner is one of Arcadia Bay's most revered and longest-standing establishments. It was placed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1978, and in 2023 was featured in renowned photographer Maxine Price-Caulfield's nationally touring exhibition Lost In Time.

Famous for classic homestyle cooking and old-fashioned service, the Two Whales is also proud to host the monthly Arcadia Bay Fishing Club awards.
